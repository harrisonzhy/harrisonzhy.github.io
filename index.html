Biography
I am Harrison Zhang (张鸿宇), a fourth-year A.B./S.M. candidate at Harvard College and researcher at MIT CSAIL. Previously, I interned at Meta (Facebook), where I helped bring up Meta's next-gen distributed AI training fabric, experimented with 2:4 sparsity for LLaMA 3 pre-training (with MSL Accelerator Enablement), and optimized new hierarchical reduction kernels based on PAT. Before that, I interned at AMD prototyping activation sparsity awareness for neural processing units. [...]
Interests
Fast AI inference and training paradigm exploration, AI accelerators and HW/SW co-design, low latency and distributed systems, robotic perception and vision, computer graphics.
